Published Games

Factory Reset

Factory Reset is a first-person game set in an abandoned robotic arms factory. As you uncover the remnants of the Sentinel Robotics Initiative, and Dr Lena Kwon, you’ll piece together your story. Navigate eerie corridors, decode hidden messages, and understand the legacy of machines designed to defend but built to destroy.

Created by: Amber Cooper, Aiden Africa, Timothy Chilezi

Play on itch.io

FPS Prototype 4

FPS Prototype 4 is a prototype game where I explore how to work with different guns and delevop an understanding of how to make first person shooter games, with high importance of UI/UX communication. This is a prototype where I used a first person shooter to investigate the importance of Visual Communication in a high-intensity game environment.

Created by: Timothy Chilezi

Play on itch.io
